# Image Generation Prompts for Gemini Web

以下のプロンプトをコピーして、Geminiのウェブインターフェース（または他の画像生成ツール）に入力して画像を生成してください。

## 1. OGP / SNSシェア画像 (og-image.png)
**プロンプト:**
> Generate a social media share image (1200x630). Modern, sleek, dark grey to purple gradient background. "Pop creator" vibe. Scattered, floating 3D-style icons representing: a game controller, a brain (AI), a frying pan (cooking), a deer silhouette (hunting), and a heart (volunteering). No text. High quality, digital art style.

## 2. ヒーローセクション背景 (hero-bg.png)
**プロンプト:**
> Generate a high-resolution website background wallpaper (1920x1080). Abstract geometric design with a dark purple and grey cooler tone. Subtle glowing light particles and cyber-tech lines. Cyberpunk or gaming aesthetic but minimalist and clean, suitable for overlaying text.

## 3. プロフィール / アバター画像 (avatar.png)
**プロンプト:**
> Generate a flat vector illustration of a friendly young man named Shin-chan. He is smiling and wearing casual, stylish clothes. He is holding a game controller in one hand and a cooking spatula in the other. Simple lines, soft colors, white background. 400x400 square.

## コンテンツカード用アイコン (200x200)

**4. ゲーム・アプリ開発 (icon-game.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). A flat, modern illustration of a game controller and smartphones. Purple and blue color scheme. Minimalist vector art style. White background.

**5. AI活用 (icon-ai.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). A modern illustration of a futuristic robot head or a digital brain. Purple and cyan neon color scheme. Tech-focused style. White background.

**6. 料理レシピ (icon-cooking.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). A warm, friendly illustration of a cooking pot or frying pan with steam rising. Orange and red color scheme. Soft, hand-drawn style. White background.

**7. 狩猟 (icon-hunting.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). A nature-themed illustration of a deer silhouette with forest trees. Green and brown earthy color scheme. Flat design. White background.

**8. ボランティア (icon-volunteer.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). An illustration of hands shaking or a heart symbol comprising many people. Pink and orange warm color scheme. Community-focused vibe. White background.

**9. その他 (icon-misc.png)**
> Generate a square icon (200x200). A playful collage of a star, a lightbulb, and a notebook. Colorful and fun pop style. White background.

## オプション (Optional)
